Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about the US visa process

What documents are required for a US tourist visa?

For a B-1/B-2 tourist visa, you typically need a valid passport, DS-160 confirmation page, application fee payment receipt, photo, travel itinerary, proof of financial support, and evidence of ties to your home country. Our advisors can provide a personalized checklist based on your specific situation.

How long does the visa application process take?

Processing times vary by visa type and current demand. Generally, you should plan for 3-5 weeks from submission to decision, though some cases may take longer. Work and specialized visas often require additional processing time. We recommend applying at least 3 months before your intended travel date.

What increases my chances of visa approval?

Strong ties to your home country, clear travel purpose, sufficient financial resources, and honest, consistent information across all documents and interviews significantly improve approval chances. Our advisors focus on strengthening these aspects of your application.

Can you guarantee my visa will be approved?

No legitimate visa service can guarantee approval, as the final decision rests with US consular officers. However, our 98% success rate demonstrates our expertise in preparing strong applications and properly equipping clients for interviews. We focus on maximizing your approval chances through meticulous preparation.
